A general ROS-wide standard for messages and services is the naming convention for messages and services.
The rule is: all messages and services should be written in Capitalised CamelCase.
Following are the guidelines for CamelCase:

  1. The only characters allowed are the alphabet (a-z,A-Z) and the digits (0-9). This means no underscores allowed!
  2. Each word starts with a Capital letter, even after a number.

This inspection checks whether or not these conditions are met, although proper spelling is left out.
